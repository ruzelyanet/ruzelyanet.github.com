About us

The Extra Mineral brand is a unique creation that combines cutting-edge cosmetic technologies with the natural mineral treasures of the Dead Sea found 429 meters below sea level. Extra Mineral's high-quality products are formulated using the the world's best skincare ingredients based on natural extracts and enriched with powerful vitamins and minerals. Our products contain none of the harsh chemicals used by other cosmetic companies Extra Mineral's exclusive product line gives pleasure and satisfaction to consumers, spas, hotels and businesses worldwide.
